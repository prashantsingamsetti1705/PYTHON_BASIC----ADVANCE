			===========================================================
						1. Arithmetic Operators
			===========================================================
=>The purpose of Arithmetic Operators is that "To Perform Arithmetic Operations such Addition, Substraction, Multiplication..etc"
=>If Two OR More Variables are connected with Arithmetic Operators then It is called Arithmetic Expression.
=>In Python Programming, we have 7 Types of Arithmetic Operators. They are given in the table
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	SLNO		SYMBOL			MEANING			EXAMPLES  a=10  b=3
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	1.				+			Addition				print(a+b)-------- 13
	
	2.				-			Substraction			print(a-b)----------7
	
	3.				*			Multiplication			print(a*b)----------30

	4.				/			Division				print(a/b)----------3.3333333333333335
							   (Float Quotient)

	5.				//			Floor Division		print(a//b)---------3
							   (Integer Quotient)

	6.				%			Modulo Division		print(a%b)---------1
								(Remainder)
								
	7.				**			Exponentiation		print(a**b)---------1000
								(power )
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
#    Here a+b, a-b, a*b, a/b, a//b, a%b, a**b are Arithmetic Expressions
